The visualization you see here is a model of the natural disasters occurring in the United States and the global temperature changing from 1965-2020. As global warming becomes more of a reality day-by-day, our team wanted to create a tool for users to explore the long-term effects of humanity's impact on our environment. Each state is colored based on the disaster count color spectrum on the right.


Source: Federal Emergency Management Agency.

Created by: Helen Li, Kalyn Bowen and Yuji Chan.
